Blueberry Smoothie

Description

285 calories, 31 grams of protein

Meal prep time: 10 mins, for 1 serving

A healthy shake made with almond milk, greek yogurt, and vanilla protein powder. A delicious way to add protein to your day.

Credits to A Sweet Pea Chef

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk or milk of choice
  • 1/2 cup full fat greek yogurt
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 3/4 cup frozen blueberries
  • 3-4 ice cubes as needed
  • 1 teaspoon raw honey (optional for added sweetness)
