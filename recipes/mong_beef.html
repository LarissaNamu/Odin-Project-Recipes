Easy Mongolian Beef

Description

303 calories, 26 grams of protein

Meal prep time: 20 mins, for 4 servings

A rich and flavorful dish. A favorite takeout made at home.

Credits to My Forking Life

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 2 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2-4 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped with green and white parts separated
  • 4 garlic cloved, chopped
  • 1 inch ginger chopped
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 3 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cover flank steak with cornstarch
  2. Heat canola oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add flank steak to frying pan in a single layer making sure the pieces are not touching. Cook on 1-2 minutes per side until browned. Cook in batched until all steak is cooked. Set aside.
  3. Add onion, whites of green onions, garlic, and ginger to skillet and stir fry for about 3 minutes, until onions have slightly softened. Add soy sauce, water, hoisin sauce, and brown sugar and stir.
  4. Add steak to pan along with green parts of the green onion. Remove from heat and serve.

Enjoy!
