Naked Chicken Burrito Bowl

Description

378 calories, 30 grams of protein

Meal prep time: 35 mins, for 4 servings

A fresh, healthy and customizable lunch thats high in protein and low in calories, featuring chicken and beans.

Credits to myprotein.com

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • 1 pinch pepper

  • 2 cans black beans(drained)
