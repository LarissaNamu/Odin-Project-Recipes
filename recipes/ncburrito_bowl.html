Naked Chicken Burrito Bowl

Description

378 calories, 30 grams of protein

Meal prep time: 35 mins, for 4 servings

A fresh, healthy and customizable lunch thats high in protein and low in calories, featuring chicken and beans.

Credits to myprotein.com

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 2 chicken breasts (cubed)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 cans black beans(drained)
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 200 grams of brown rice (50 grams per serving)

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large non-stick pan and add onion. Fry for a few minutes to soften and add crushed garlic.
  2. Add cubed chicken to pan, season with salt, pepper, and paprika, then stir well. Cool for 5-10 minutes until chicken is completely cooked through.
  3. Tip the drained beans and salsa, mixing well to combine in the pan. Leave to cook through for a few minutes and then portion up with the cooked rice in meal prep containers.

Enjoy!
