Korean Beef Bowl

Description

398 calories, 29 grams of protein

Meal prep time: 30 mins, for 4 servings

Super fast and delicious simplified meal. A great dinner choice with high protein!

Credits to Cooking Classy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoon honey*
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon dark brown/white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoon lime juice
  • 3 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon water
  • 2 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 18 oz 85% lean ground beef
  • 1/3 cup chopped green onions (white portion)
  • 1 tablespoon peeled and minced fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 2 teaspoon water (optional)
  • chopped green onions (green portion) for garnish
  • 2 cups cooked white or brown rice

*can be replaced by the sugar

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix honey, sugar, sesame oil, lime juice, soy sauce, and water. Set aside.
  2. Preheat a 12-inch skillet over medium high heat. Crumble beef into chunks. Add oil to skillet and beef chunks.
  3. Let beef cook undisturbed for 2 to 3 minutes until nicely browned on bottom. Sprinkle green onions (white part), garlic, ginger, and optional red pepper flakes.
  4. Toss and break up beef and continue to cook until beef til fully brown. Dab up excess fat with paper towel.
  5. Pour in honey mixture and reduce heat to medium. Add cornstarch mixture if you want the sauce thickened. Simmer beef with sauce, stirring frequently for 1 minute. Adjust taste with soy sauce and salt to preference.
  6. Serve warm over rice, sprinkle with green onions (green part).

Note: nutricion facts accounts for meat and rice only, other sides and toppings not included

Enjoy!
