Garlic Butter Chicken Meatballs with Cauliflower Rice

Description

400 calories, 25.8 grams of protein

Meal prep time: 25 mins, for 4 servings

An easy meal prep featuring delicious chicken meatballs and cauliflower rice. Perfect for a high protein low calorie diet.

Credits to eatwell101

Ingredients

  • 450 g of ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup of shredded lowfat cheese
  • 4 cloves of garlic, grated + 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon red crushed chili pepper flakes
  • 1 bouillon cube (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsely or cilantro
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce of choice
  • 1 packet of riced cauliflower

Steps

  1. Make cauliflower rice with a large grater or food processor. Transfer to a shallow plate with 1/2 cup water and cook covered in the microwave for 4 minutes
  2. In a large bowl combine chicken, cheese, grated garlic, italian seasoning, bouillon cube, chili flakes, chopped parsely, and black pepper. Mix well and form medium balls.
  3. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in large skillet over medium-low heat. Cook the chicken meatballs for 8-10 minutes on all sides until browned and cooked through. While cooking, baste the meatball with the butter and juices.
  4. In the same skillet melt the remaining butter, add lemon juice, chicken stock, hot sauce, minced garlice, parsley, and red pepper flakes. Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring regularly until the sauce had reduced. Adjust seasoning as needed.
  5. Divide cauliflower rice into meal prep containers. Top with chicken meatballs and garnish with lemon slices. Drizzle a little of the sauce over the meatballs and rice. Reheat your chicken meatballs meal prep quickly in the microwave when ready to eat.

Enjoy!
